Coffee

“Blacker than a moonless night, hotter and more bitter than hell itself, that is… coffee”

Diego Armando

I have suprised even myself. In previous times, I can count the amount of times I drank boutique drinks in a year on one hand. After interning at a bank for only 5 months, it is safe to say that I have exceeded the amount of drinks I have drank in my entire lifetime. This is greatly concerning, as it is clashing against my anti-capitalist rhetoric, and also against my minimal expenditure lifestyle. Let us do a simple cost analysis on how much I have spent on drinks in the past 5 months.

 Amount of time worked = 21 Weeks

 Actual time in office = 17 Weeks

 Average of 2 drinks a week.

 Average cost of drink = RM10-RM 20 so we'll take it as RM15

 15 * 2 * 17 = RM510

And I think that is a truly absurd amount of money that I have spent on coffee and chocolate. Just by cutting that out of my expenditure I can probably eat comfortably for at least a month. However, I have to note that I did not actually pay that much. My kind sisters and coworkers keep buying me drinks. I am puzzled as to why. I’m not very talkative and outgoing. People seem to like me. I am so confounded. Nevertheless, I am grateful. Another benefit to coffee time is the talks over coffee that you have. People live such interesting lives. Does this make the around RM500 investment worth it? Nope.

Could have bought a telecaster.
